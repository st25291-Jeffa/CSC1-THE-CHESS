Pawn

There are 8 pawns on the board going from a2
to h2 if you're white and a7 to h8 if you're
black, they can only move forward and take pieces
diagonally, and on first move they can choose to
move 2 squares up or 1 square up.

Rook
There are 2 rooks on a board, which can
only move diagonally, its starting position
is at a1 and h1 if you're black and a8 and
h8 if you're white

Bishop
There are 2 bishops on a board they can
only move diagonally as to its
starting position which is c1 and f1
if you are white and c8 and f8 if your black.
